## 🎵 Features & Additions

### 1. Quran Background Audio Recitation Service
- **Implementation:** Added a custom AndroidX `MediaSessionService` named [QuranAudioService](file:///D:/AI/Projects%20With%20AI/Islamic%20App/Muslim%20Companion%20V1/muslim-companion/app/src/main/java/com/example/audio/QuranAudioService.kt).
- **Functionality:** Scopes and manages a global `ExoPlayer` instance, enabling uninterrupted audio recitation of the Holy Quran even when the app is in the background or the screen is locked.
- **Media Controls:** Displays a system-native **MediaStyle Notification** with:
  - Play, pause, skip-next, skip-previous buttons
  - Recitation progress bar with seeking
  - Live metadata tracking (Surah name, Reciter name)

### 2. Material 3 Home Screen Player Widget
- **Implementation:** Added [QuranPlayerWidgetProvider](file:///D:/AI/Projects%20With%20AI/Islamic%20App/Muslim%20Companion%20V1/muslim-companion/app/src/main/java/com/example/notifications/QuranPlayerWidgetProvider.kt).
- **Aesthetics:** Styled with a premium deep-green Material 3 container shape matching the app theme.
- **Controls:** Embedded buttons directly into the home screen widget to play/pause, skip-next, and skip-previous recitations, along with a tap-shortcut to quickly open the Quran reader.

### 3. Authentic Adhan MP3 Assets
- **Upgrade:** Replaced default placeholder MP3 files with high-quality, authentic Adhan audios:
  - `first_adhan.mp3` (updated)
  - `full_adhan.mp3` (extended to a 5.3MB high-fidelity file)

## 🛠️ Bug Fixes & Refactoring

### 1. Prayer Notification ANR & Reliability Fixes
- **Location:** [PrayerNotificationReceiver](file:///D:/AI/Projects%20With%20AI/Islamic%20App/Muslim%20Companion%20V1/muslim-companion/app/src/main/java/com/example/notifications/PrayerNotificationReceiver.kt)
- **Fix:** Swapped the blocking `runBlocking` Room DB query with a non-blocking coroutine inside `goAsync()` to avoid **ANR (Application Not Responding)** risk during background execution.
- **Notification ID Collision:** Updated notification IDs to prevent newer prayer alarms from overwriting previous ones.

### 2. Consolidated Calculation Method Mappers
- **Change:** Deleted duplicate and unused data-layer mapper `com.example.data.mapper.MethodMapper` in favor of the domain-level [CalculationMethodMapper](file:///D:/AI/Projects%20With%20AI/Islamic%20App/Muslim%20Companion%20V1/muslim-companion/app/src/main/java/com/example/domain/CalculationMethodMapper.kt).
- **Refactoring:** Routed [CompanionRepository](file:///D:/AI/Projects%20With%20AI/Islamic%20App/Muslim%20Companion%20V1/muslim-companion/app/src/main/java/com/example/data/repository/CompanionRepository.kt) calls to use the unified domain-level calculation method mapping logic.
